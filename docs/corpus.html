Corpus Analysis: Radio Plays Comparison

For this corpus assignment, I examined the radio plays “Apple Tree” and “Horror Movie Radio Spots.” In Voyant Tools, I found that the two corpora were extremely different. Apple Tree repeated names like Frank and Megan, while the horror radio spots repeated sound cues and voice directions. In AntConc, the 3-gram “frank ashurst narrates” appeared most often in Apple Tree, whereas “then in bg” was the most frequent in Horror Movie Radio Spots. These differences highlight how narrative vs. sound-driven radio plays emphasize completely different types of language.
